About Us

The Munder Difflin Company has been family owned and operated since its establishment in 1976 by Saddie and Munder Difflin. This is a privately owned family-run operation. The whole family is in the paper business. This includes the Difflin sons: Steve, Stanley, Stephon, Stewart, the Difflin daughters: Mindy and Mandy, as well as the 11 Difflin grandchildren (who will not be named individually for the sake of space)! This creates a comfortable environment for long-term clients and displays our commitment to our customers for generations to come.
